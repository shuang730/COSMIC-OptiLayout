  <section>

    <h2>Overview</h2>

    <p>

      COSMIC OptiLayout helps users build, visualize, and modify 2D optical layouts with interactive ray tracing,

      adjustable optical components, measurement tools, and export options for documentation.

    </p>


    <img class="screenshot" src="screenshots/interface.png" alt="COSMIC OptiLayout screenshot">

  </section>


  <section>

    <h2>Features</h2>


    <div class="features">

      <div class="card">

        <h3>Real-time Ray Tracing</h3>

        <p>Create parallel beams and point sources with adjustable angles, beam width, ray count, and colors.</p>

      </div>


      <div class="card">

        <h3>Optical Components</h3>

        <p>Supports lenses, objectives, mirrors, beam splitters, pinholes, cameras, and surfaces.</p>

      </div>


      <div class="card">

        <h3>Editable Properties</h3>

        <p>Modify position, rotation, diameter, focal length, NA, aperture, and other optical parameters.</p>

      </div>


      <div class="card">

        <h3>Measurement Tools</h3>

        <p>Measure point-to-point and surface-to-surface distances directly in the layout.</p>

      </div>


      <div class="card">

        <h3>Export Figures</h3>

        <p>Save optical layouts as PNG or PDF for reports, presentations, and documentation.</p>

      </div>


      <div class="card">

        <h3>Productivity Tools</h3>

        <p>Includes undo/redo, save/load, box selection, alignment tools, zoom, pan, and view lock.</p>

      </div>

    </div>

  </section>
